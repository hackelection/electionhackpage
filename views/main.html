1. Crowdfund the £500

2. Crowdsource the 10 signatures

3. Maintain a campaign page

4. Fill out all the necessary forms, digitally

Why we're doing this

In this digital age the voting system in the UK and it’s application has become fairly outdated. The purpose of “hackelection” is make it practical, and allow the citizens to use modern tools to increase their participation. “Don’t vote for an MP, become one” enables anyone, who is determined enough, to run for MP. The purpose of the campaign is to show people how easy it is to register as an MP, and how simple and accessible the process can be made for everyone.
